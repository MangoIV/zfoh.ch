Niklas Hambüchen: Writing a tool to graph job postings on r/haskell

Thursday 12 January, 19:00

Merry Christmas and happy new year to all HaskellerZ! We hope to see you at the first meetup of the new year to kick off after the holidays!

For years, people have been complaining that the number of Haskell jobs is decreasing, while others have contended that open positions for Haskell developers are only increasing. In this talk, Niklas Hambüchen will aim to put this debate to rest by showing us how to use Haskell to write a small tool that will graph the job postings on r/haskell over time.

As usual, we will be retiring for drinks, food and discussion after the event.

Target audience: Beginner/Intermediate Haskell
