Zurihac 2024: Organizer Meering

Friday 10 November, 19:00

Every year in Summer, the Zurich Friends of Haskell and OST organize ZuriHac, a three-day Haskell hackathon and conference. We're always looking for volunteers to help organize, so if you want to contribute, or are even just curious about the event, we invite you to attend this first organizer meeting.

Even though this isn't an "official" HaskellerZ talk, the usual avenues for drinks and socializing will remain open after the event.
